      Setup

      • Players: 6-20 participants sit (approximately) in a circle.
      • Roles: Each player receives a secret role ("character") from this list, either good (Townsfolk, Outsider) or evil (Demon, Minion). There are no duplicate characters.
      • Storyteller: One player acts as the Storyteller, managing the game and enforcing rules.

      Game Structure

      The game consists of alternating night and day phases:

      • Night Phase: Players with abilities use them e.g. the Demon uses their ability to eliminate a player.
      • Day Phase: Discussion (private and public) and deduction occur, ending with a vote to execute a suspect.

      Objective

      • Good Team: Wins by executing the Demon. Focuses on gathering information to achieve this.
      • Evil Team: Wins when only two players remain, one being the Demon. Aims to deceive players (to stay hidden) and eliminate players each night.

      Additional Rules

      • Deceased players keep their aligment (good/evil) and can still participate in discussions but lose their character's ability and can only vote one more time for the rest of the game.
      • Character-specific rules supersede rules specified here.
